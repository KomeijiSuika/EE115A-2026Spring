### 2⃣ Ch9 — MOS Differential Pair / Active Load

- ⭐ 9.14 / 9.15 / 9.16 — MOS differential pair design（9.14 必做）
    
    **原题定位：** `Ch9_MOS_differential_pair_problems.pdf`，截图页 2。  
    
    **9.14 关键词：** “Design a MOS differential amplifier to operate from ±1-V power supplies...”  
    
    **9.15 关键词：** “Select the value of $V_{OV}$ so that ... steers the current ...”  
    
    **9.16 关键词：** NMOS differential amplifier, equal drain resistors, differential gain.  
    
    **为什么做：** 9.14 是你前面已经问过的 power supplies / dissipate / equilibrium 那题；最适合练「缺方程时扫 spec」。

- ⭐ 9.85 / 9.88 / 9.92 — current-mirror-loaded MOS differential pair
    
    **9.85 原题定位：** `Ch9_active_load_problems.pdf`，截图页 2。  
    
    **题目关键词：** differential amplifier of Fig. 9.32(a), short-circuit transconductance, load resistance, estimate $R_o$ and $A_d$。  
    
    **9.88 原题定位：** `Ch9_active_load_problems.pdf`，截图页 3。  
    
    **题目关键词：** current-mirror-loaded differential amplifier, all transistors characterized by $k'(W/L)$ and $|V_A|$, find bias current for a target gain.

    **为什么做：** 这组比 9.55 更符合“综合题”：先定 $G_m$，再定 $R_o$，再合成 $A_d$。
